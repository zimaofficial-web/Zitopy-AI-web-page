Workflow

Automate your sales in three simple steps

No complicated setups. PlugToCart integrates directly with your WhatsApp Business number to automate your sales funnel.

💬

1. Customer Messages You

Customers query prices, ask about stock, or send orders on WhatsApp. Our AI understands their intent instantly, even in Pidgin or local slangs.

💳

2. Autopilot Checkout

The assistant checks stock, compiles the order, calculates delivery fees, and sends a secure checkout link with local payment options (Card, Transfer, Mobile Money).

📦

3. Staff Notification

As soon as payment is confirmed, your staff is alerted via their private channel or dashboard. Inventory is updated, and delivery details are ready for dispatch.
